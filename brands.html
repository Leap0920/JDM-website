TOYOTA

Toyota Motor Corporation is a Japanese multinational automotive manufacturer, headquartered in Toyota City, Aichi, Japan, and is the largest automobile manufacturer in the world

MITSUBISHI

Mitsubishi Group is a conglomerate of autonomous Japanese multinational companies, founded by Yatarō Iwasaki in 1870, operating in a variety of industries including automotive, chemical, electronics, and financial services

MAZDA

Mazda Motor Corporation, also known simply as Mazda, is a Japanese multinational automotive manufacturer headquartered in Fuchū, Hiroshima, Japan, founded on January 30, 1920

Nissan Motor Co., Ltd., commonly known as Nissan, is a Japanese multinational automobile manufacturer headquartered in Yokohama, Kanagawa Prefecture, Japan

HONDA

Honda Motor Co., Ltd. is a Japanese public multinational conglomerate manufacturer of automobiles, motorcycles, and power equipment, headquartered in Minato, Tokyo, Japan

SUBARU

Subaru is the automobile manufacturing division of Japanese transportation conglomerate Subaru Corporation, known for its use of a boxer engine layout in most vehicles above 1,500 cc and its standard Symmetrical All Wheel Drive drive-train layou

Suzuki Motor Corporation is a Japanese multinational mobility manufacturer headquartered in Hamamatsu, Shizuoka, Japan, manufacturing automobiles, motorcycles, all-terrain vehicles (ATVs), outboard marine engines, wheelchairs and a variety of other small internal combustion engines

DAIHATSU

Daihatsu Motor Co., Ltd., commonly known as Daihatsu, is a Japanese automobile manufacturer and one of the oldest surviving Japanese internal combustion engine manufacturers, headquartered in Ikeda, Osaka Prefecture

Isuzu Motors Ltd., commonly known as Isuzu, is a Japanese multinational automobile manufacturer headquartered in Yokohama, Kanagawa Prefecture, Japan, whose principal activity is the production, marketing, and sale of Isuzu commercial vehicles and diesel engines

LEXUS

Lexus is the luxury vehicle division of the Japanese automaker Toyota, marketed in more than 90 countries worldwide, and is Japan’s largest-selling make of premium cars
